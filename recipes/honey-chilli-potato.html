Honey Chilli Potato

Description

Chilli Potato is a popular Indo-Chinese starter that is very addictive. Chilli Potato combines the flavors of Chinese cuisine with Indian spices.

Chilli Potato is a delicious snack or appetizer that includes crispy fried potatoes tossed in a flavorful sauce. Chilli Potato can be served as a starter or snack or even as a side dish for fried rice. It is also called as Aloo Chilli or Chilli Aloo.

Ingredients

  • Potato - Choose medium sized potatoes. Rinse it well and then peel off the skin. You can use regular Indian potatoes or russet potatoes or even baby potatoes for this recipe.
  • Flour - Cornflour and rice flour is used for making the batter. You can replace rice flour with maida too.
  • Sauces - Soya sauce, chilli Sauce and tomato Sauce is used. You can replace Chilli sauce with schezwan sauce too.
  • Vegetables - Ginger, garlic, onion and capsicum are used. Spring onion gives a great flavor to this dish.
  • Spice powders - Pepper powder is used.
