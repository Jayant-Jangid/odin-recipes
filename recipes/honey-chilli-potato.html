Honey Chilli Potato

Description

Chilli Potato is a popular Indo-Chinese starter that is very addictive. Chilli Potato combines the flavors of Chinese cuisine with Indian spices.

Chilli Potato is a delicious snack or appetizer that includes crispy fried potatoes tossed in a flavorful sauce. Chilli Potato can be served as a starter or snack or even as a side dish for fried rice. It is also called as Aloo Chilli or Chilli Aloo.

Ingredients

  • Potato - Choose medium sized potatoes. Rinse it well and then peel off the skin. You can use regular Indian potatoes or russet potatoes or even baby potatoes for this recipe.
  • Flour - Cornflour and rice flour is used for making the batter. You can replace rice flour with maida too.
  • Sauces - Soya sauce, chilli Sauce and tomato Sauce is used. You can replace Chilli sauce with schezwan sauce too.
  • Vegetables - Ginger, garlic, onion and capsicum are used. Spring onion gives a great flavor to this dish.
  • Spice powders - Pepper powder is used.

Steps

  1. Rinse potatoes well, peel off the skin. Slice them first. I used 4 medium sized potatoes.
  2. Cut into long strips. Repeat to finish.
  3. Keep it immersed in water until use.
  4. Boil 3 cups water, when it starts to boil add salt to taste along with drained potatoes. Cook for around 3-5 minutes.
  5. Strain and remove the potatoes. Spread it on a plate and set aside to cool.
  6. To a mixing bowl add ¼ cup rice flour, ¼ cup cornflour along with salt to taste, ¼ teaspoon pepper powder. Add little water.
  7. Mix well to form a slightly thick batter like this.Mix well to form a slightly thick batter like this.
  8. Add few potato pieces and coat with the batter. Add in batches and coat with batter.
  9. Add to preheated oil and fry until golden brown. While frying if it sticks, gently break it else you can break it after frying also. Repeat to finish.
  10. Heat 1 tablespoon oil add 1 tablespoon garlic, 1 teaspoon ginger and 1 long green chilli. Saute until golden.
  11. Add ½ cup onion and ½ cup capsicum.
  12. Give a quick saute in medium high flame. Then add 1 teaspoon soya sauce, 1.5 teaspoon chilli sauce, 2 to 3 tablespoon tomato sauce, salt to taste and ½ teaspoon pepper powder. Finally add 1 teaspoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoon water.
  13. Give a quick mix then add ¼ cup water to it.
  14. Give a quick mix and let it boil for a minute.
  15. Add fried potatoes.
  16. Give a quick toss until the sauce coats the potatoes well. Finally add 1 tablespoon spring onion, 1 teaspoon vinegar, give a quick mix and switch off.

Serve hot!
